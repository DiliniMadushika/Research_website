AI-Powered Research Project

EduGuide.

AI-Powered Intelligent Education Support System

EduGuide is an AI-powered intelligent education support system designed for Sri Lankan O/L students. It predicts attendance risks, academic performance, stress levels, and generates personalized learning paths using machine learning and data analytics.

View Research Explore System

Attendance Prediction

Forecast student attendance trends using time-series analysis and LSTM models.

Academic Risk Prediction

Classify academic risk levels (Low / Medium / High) with Gradient Boosting.

Adaptive Learning Paths

Generate personalized learning journeys through K-Means clustering.

Stress Level Prediction

Detect student stress indicators early using Random Forest classifiers.

Research Domain

Domain & Methodology

Explore the research foundation, identified gaps, and the scientific approach behind EduGuide.

01

Literature Survey

Foundations of our research

Intelligent Education Systems

Review of existing intelligent tutoring systems, adaptive learning platforms, and education management solutions deployed globally.

Learning Analytics

Study of data-driven approaches to understand and optimize student learning processes, engagement patterns, and outcome prediction.

AI in Education

Exploration of machine learning, deep learning, and data mining techniques applied to educational contexts for predictive analytics.

02

Research Gap

Identified shortcomings in existing solutions

Lack of Personalized Support

Current systems offer generic learning paths, failing to adapt to individual student needs, abilities, and learning speeds.

No Early Risk Detection

Absence of proactive mechanisms to identify students at risk of poor attendance, academic failure, or elevated stress before it escalates.

No Integrated AI System

Existing tools address individual concerns in isolation — no unified system combines attendance, performance, stress, and learning analytics.

03

Research Problem

The core challenge we address

Students lack early identification of academic risk, attendance issues, and stress due to the absence of intelligent, data-driven systems capable of holistic analysis and proactive intervention in the Sri Lankan O/L education context.

04

Research Objectives

What EduGuide aims to achieve

RO1

Attendance Trend Prediction

Develop a time-series forecasting model to predict student attendance patterns using LSTM neural networks.

RO2

Academic Risk Classification

Build a classification system to categorize students into Low, Medium, and High risk tiers based on academic indicators.

RO3

Adaptive Learning Path Generation

Design a clustering-based engine that generates personalized learning paths tailored to individual student profiles.

RO4

Student Stress Prediction

Implement a predictive model to detect and classify student stress levels from behavioral and academic data.

05

Methodology

Our scientific approach

Data Collection

  • Attendance records
  • Academic results
  • Behavioral data
  • Weather patterns

Preprocessing

  • Data cleaning
  • Feature engineering
  • Normalization
  • Train/test split

ML Models

  • LSTM — Attendance
  • Gradient Boosting — Risk
  • K-Means — Learning Paths
  • Random Forest — Stress

Evaluation

  • Accuracy metrics
  • Precision / Recall
  • F1 Score
  • K-fold validation
